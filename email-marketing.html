Why It's Different

Email Built for Trades — Not Retail, Not SaaS.

✓
Your Past Clients Are Your Best Leads

A homeowner who hired you once trusts you already. Staying in their inbox with relevant content and timely reminders costs a fraction of acquiring a new lead — and converts at a much higher rate.

✓
Seasonal Timing Is Everything

We plan campaigns around your trade's natural demand windows — not generic "best practice" calendars. HVAC emails go out before summer. Roofing follow-ups hit after storm season. Timing is strategy.

✓
Referral Requests Built In

Happy customers refer people — but only if you ask. Our sequences include well-timed referral requests that generate new business from your existing relationships without being pushy.

✓
No Templates That Look Like Templates

We write every email to sound like it came from a real person running a real business — not a mass blast. Contractors win with authenticity, and your emails should reflect that.

✓
Integrates With Your Other Channels

Email works alongside your SEO, PPC, and social media — amplifying everything else. A lead that finds you via Google gets nurtured via email. A PPC click that doesn't convert gets followed up automatically.

✓
Owned Channel — You Control It

Social media algorithms change. Ad costs rise. Your email list is yours — no platform can take it from you. It's the most durable marketing asset a contractor can build.
